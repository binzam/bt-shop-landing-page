Project Features

Product Catalog

A comprehensive product catalog that allows users to browse and search for products. It includes product images, descriptions, prices, and other relevant information.

Shopping Cart

A virtual shopping cart allows users to add products they wish to purchase. It displays the selected items, quantities, prices, and provide options for editing or removing items.

Product Filtering

Robust filering functionality to help users find products quickly. Filtering options based on categories and other attributes can enhance the user experience.

About Project

Creating bt-shop has been an incredibly fulfilling journey for me, and it all started with a personal experience that ignited my passion for this project. Lately, I found myself struggling to find a user-friendly online shopping platform that catered to my diverse needs. The frustration of sifting through countless websites and dealing with subpar customer service left me longing for a better solution. That's when I realized the immense potential of creating an ecommerce app that could address these pain points and provide a seamless shopping experience for everyone.

The idea took root, and I embarked on this project with determination and a clear vision. I wanted to build an ecommerce web app that not only offered an unparalleled selection of products but also prioritized user-friendliness.

As a student at Holberton School, this project served as a Portfolio Project that allowed me to showcase my skills and creativity. The rigorous curriculum and support from my mentors at Holberton School played a crucial role in shaping the app's development.
